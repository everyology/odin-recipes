Classic Waffles

Description

A waffle is a battercake cooked between two iron plates, resulting in its characteristic grid pattern. Waffles are commonly enjoyed with syrup and butter.

Ingredients (for 5 servings)

  • All-purpose flour: 2 cups
  • Salt: 1 teaspoon
  • Baking powder: 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon
  • White sugar: 2 tablespoons
  • Eggs: 2
  • Warm milk: 1 ½ cups
  • Butter (melted): ¼ cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon
  • Vanilla extract: 1 teaspoon

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix together flour, salt, baking powder and sugar; set aside. Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature.
  2. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs. Stir in the milk, butter and vanilla. Pour the milk mixture into the flour mixture; beat until blended.
  3. Ladle the batter into a preheated waffle iron. Cook the waffles until golden and crisp. Serve immediately.
